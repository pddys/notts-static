Sign Language Interpreted (Integrated) Performance:

British Sign Language (BSL) interpreted performances are designed to provide access to theatre for people who use BSL. On Friday 22 February, there will be a ‘semi-integrated’ interpretation, where the interpreter is in costume and up close with the actors, allowing for a more immersive experience.
